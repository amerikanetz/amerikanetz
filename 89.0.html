District of Mississippi - Port of New Orleans

I, Herm. Baetjer Master or Commander of the Bremen ship Louisiana, do solemnly, sincerely and truly swear, that the within List, signed by me and now delivered to the Collector of this District, contains the names of all the Passengers, taken on board the said ship Louisiana at the Port of Bremen or at any time since, and that all matters therein set forth are, according to the best of my knowledge and belief just and true, I do further swear, that two of said passengers have died on the voyage.

Sworn before me, this 23 day of Oct. 1850, [signed] Collector

Annotations:
This is a special list as most of the Prussian people came from one particular part of Prussia: the district of Herford. Of the 220 passengers listed, 108 came from this area in Eastern Westphalia. For most of these people additional data is avaiable: birth, marriage, death dates, places of residence etc. Sometimes it is not known where these families settled in the USA and what became of them.
Names underlined mark families. The spelling of some names is corrected. Place names underlined are added to the list. No corrections of the spelling are made to the places of origin and the occupation.
